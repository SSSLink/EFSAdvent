PNP2 (Panel rewriting NPC2)
Causes a tile on the entire area to be converted into another tile.

Variable 1a:
Variable that activates the tile to be changed.

Variable 2+3:
Value of the tile that should replace another.

Variable 3+4:
Value of the tile to be replaced by another.
